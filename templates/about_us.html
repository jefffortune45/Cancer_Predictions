Welcome to Cancer Prediction, the result of the commitment and determination of a group of computer science students at Espoir University in Calvary Chapel. Our story begins with a deep conviction: technology can be a catalyst for meaningful change in healthcare.

As students passionate about the power of technology, we were deeply touched by the devastating impact of breast cancer, skin cancer and heart attacks on so many lives. This realization was the starting point for our commitment to finding an innovative solution to facilitate the early diagnosis of these diseases.
