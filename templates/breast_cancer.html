Common signs of breast cancer and what to look for. What are breast cancer symptoms?

  1. 1. Consult an oncology specialist

    Make an appointment with an oncologist or breast cancer specialist to discuss treatment options, get detailed information about the disease and develop a care plan tailored to your situation.

  2. 2. Be well informed

    Learn about the different stages of breast cancer, available treatments, possible side effects and post-treatment care. Understanding the disease can help you make informed decisions.

  3. 3. Surround yourself with a support network

    Share your emotions, questions and concerns with family and friends, and join support groups to meet others going through similar experiences. Emotional support is essential throughout your journey.

  4. 4. Take care of yourself physically and mentally

    Adopt a healthy lifestyle including a balanced diet, regular exercise and relaxation to reduce stress and promote your overall well-being.

  5. 5. Take part in your treatment

    Take an active part in discussions with your medical team. Ask questions, raise concerns and discuss treatment options so you can make informed decisions.

  6. 6. Follow treatment and medical recommendations

    Strictly adhere to medical appointments, treatment sessions and specific recommendations from your medical team to optimize your recovery.

  7. 7. Stay positive and hopeful

    Keep a positive attitude as much as possible, surround yourself with positive people and stay focused on your recovery. The mind can play an important role in your well-being.
